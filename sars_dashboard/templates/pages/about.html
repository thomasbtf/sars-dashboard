About

SARS-CoV-2 Sequencing

Data Science Research Group

The Data Science Lab is part of the Institute for Artificial Intelligence in Medicine (IKIM) at University Hospital Essen, Medical Faculty University of Duisburg-Essen. We are focused on efficient, reproducible and sustainable Data Science and Microbiome Analysis.

Our group aims for honor the principles of Data FAIRness as well as reproducible science via the use of workflow technology.
